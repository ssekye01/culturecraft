Adeola Adebayo
Yoruba Heritage Artist

Adeola’s work celebrates Yoruba ceremony and rhythm – from beaded crowns to moonlit drum circles – blending traditional symbols with a soft, contemporary palette.

French Studio Painter

Julien paints quiet corners of Parisian life – bookends, café ceramics, and twilight façades – turning everyday objects into keepsake artworks.
